# Product 001 Charter - PlayPatch Kids

Status: MVP prototype

## One-Line Product

PlayPatch Kids is a paid, no-ads activity app that helps parents turn screen
time into short real-world play missions.

## Target Buyer

Parents of children aged 4-8 who want quick, safe, low-prep activities at home.

## User

Child with parent nearby.

## Why This Can Be Sellable

- Clear parent pain: "What can my child do now that is safe and useful?"
- Paid-app fit: no ads, no tracking, offline-friendly, simple activity packs.
- Mobile fit: short sessions, big controls, local progress, parent gate.

## MVP Scope

- Activity filters by age, time, and energy.
- Activity cards with supplies, steps, safety note, and focus timer.
- Favorites and patches stored locally.
- Included starter pack plus parent-gated premium pack previews.
- Parent settings for optional sound cues and calm endings.
- Parent gate with privacy/safety explanation.
- PWA shell as the first deployment path before native mobile wrapping.

## Excluded From MVP

- Child accounts.
- Social sharing.
- Chat.
- Camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photo uploads.
- Third-party ads or analytics.
- In-app purchase implementation.

## Safety And Privacy Guardrails

- No personal child data collection.
- No behavioral tracking.
- No external links in child flow.
- Future purchases and settings stay behind a parent gate.
- Activities must be safe, low-prep, and require normal household objects.

## Future Monetization

- Paid app download.
- Optional parent-gated content packs later.
- School/parent bundle only after privacy review.
