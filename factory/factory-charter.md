# Factory Charter

Status: Draft baseline for Factory 1.0

## Purpose

Factory is the private internal operating system for BuildPodFactory. It exists to create future SaaS, mobile, and internal software products in a controlled, evidence-based way.

## Non-Goals

- Factory is not the product to sell.
- Factory does not build a product until the human owner approves a Product Build Charter.
- Factory does not add tools, models, or frameworks without a recorded tool decision.

## Operating Principles

- Product ideas come from the human owner.
- No source means assumption.
- No evidence means not done.
- No approval means no high-impact decision is accepted.
- Keep Factory lean before adding automation.
