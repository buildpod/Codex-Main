# Product Quality Standard

Status: Draft baseline for Factory 1.0

## Purpose

Define the minimum bar for a sellable product produced by Factory.

## Sellable Product Bar

- Solves a specific buyer/user problem.
- Has a clear onboarding path.
- Has polished core workflows, not only screens.
- Handles empty, loading, error, and permission states.
- Protects customer data.
- Has basic analytics or event tracking for important actions.
- Has support/contact path.
- Has pricing/billing plan if sold directly.
- Has release notes and known limitations.
- Has backup/recovery expectation for customer data.

## Release Blockers

- Confusing primary workflow.
- Broken auth or access control.
- Unhandled critical errors.
- Exposed secrets.
- Missing data backup/recovery plan for persistent products.
- No evidence for core workflow testing.

## Rule

Factory optimizes for useful, trusted, sellable products, not demos.
