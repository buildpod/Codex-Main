# Privacy And Legal Baseline

Status: Draft baseline for Factory 1.0

## Purpose

Identify legal/privacy items that products may need before selling or releasing.

## Product Checklist

- Privacy policy needed?
- Terms of service needed?
- Data processing agreement needed?
- Cookie/tracking notice needed?
- Refund/cancellation policy needed?
- Customer support contact needed?
- Data retention/deletion process needed?
- Regulated data involved?
- Third-party subprocessors involved?

## Rule

Factory may draft legal/privacy materials, but human owner or qualified professional must approve them before external use.
