# Support And Operations Baseline

Status: Draft baseline for Factory 1.0

## Purpose

Define minimum operations expectations for sellable products.

## Required Before Selling

- Support contact or support workflow.
- Basic monitoring/logging plan.
- Backup and recovery expectation.
- Customer data export/deletion expectation.
- Incident response contact.
- Known limitations list.
- Release notes.

## Operational Risks

- No one knows when the product is down.
- Customer data cannot be recovered.
- Users cannot report issues.
- Product changes are not communicated.

## Rule

Sellable products need support and recovery planning, even when MVP.
